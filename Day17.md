# Day 17 — 2026-07-14

## Topic: Dashboard Integration Testing

I spent today testing my work together with Cheema, who is building the dashboard. We added a few test transactions and checked that the totals, the charts, and everything on the dashboard updated correctly right after. This kind of testing between two different parts of the app is called integration testing, and it really helps catch bugs that you would never find by testing your own part alone.

**What I learned today:** Bugs often hide in the connection between two features, not inside just one feature alone.
